THE BLOOM COLLECTIVE

The old world fell silent. No war. No warning. Just… stillness.

In the canopy of an ancient tree, a small community begins again.
You are their conscience. Their consensus. Their voice.

Guide them. Grow. Bloom.

How to Lead

A moment of guidance before the work begins.

🧑 Community Tab — Assign survivors to roles. Foragers gather food, Gardeners grow food and compost, Crafters and Researchers build toward the Landmark.
🔨 Build Tab — Spend resources to construct structures on the Great Tree. Each building provides passive bonuses every day.
🗺️ Expeditions Tab — Send 1-2 survivors to explore distant locations for resources and story. They'll be away for days.
⚖️ Dilemmas — Moral choices will arise. You won't know the exact consequences — only the voices of your people. Choose wisely.
🌸 Bloom Score — Your ecological harmony. High bloom = abundant resources. Low bloom = short-term gain, long-term ruin.
🏛️ Goal — Complete the Community Landmark by assigning Crafters & Researchers. Press End Day → to advance.
