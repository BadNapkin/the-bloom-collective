THE BLOOM COLLECTIVE

The old world fell silent. No war. No warning. Just… stillness.

In the canopy of an ancient tree, a small community begins again.
You are their conscience. Their consensus. Their voice.

Guide them. Grow. Find The Way.

How to Lead

A moment of guidance before the work begins.

🧑 Community Tab Assign survivors to roles. Foragers gather food, Gardeners grow food & compost, Crafters build the Landmark, Researchers advance research & discover supplies, Medics accelerate healing. Working drains health.
😴 Rest & Recovery Survivors recover health & morale passively overnight. Build Sleeping Quarters to double recovery. Build a Recreation Area for daily morale. Unassigned survivors rest without fatigue.
🔨 Build Tab Spend resources to construct structures on the Great Tree. Each building provides passive bonuses every day.
🗺️ Expeditions Tab Send 1-2 survivors to explore distant locations. Expeditions are exhausting — departing survivors lose health. They'll be away for days.
⚖️ Dilemmas Moral choices will arise — but they come from your survivors, shaped by their traits. Choosing one person's idea over another builds bonds and creates rivalries. Fate is fickle — even the "right" choice can backfire.
⚖️ The Balance Your community walks between Harmony (nature, renewal, patience) and Industry (output, construction, speed). Neither extreme is purely good or bad. Finding The Way is the challenge.
🐾 Pets Build the Animal Nursery to rescue wild animals. Pets help with expeditions, research, foraging, and morale. Up to 4 companions.
🤝 Relationships Survivors form bonds through shared work and expedition partnerships. During dilemmas, each choice is proposed by a survivor — siding with someone strengthens your bond but may create friction with others. Friends boost morale; rivals drain it.
🏛️ Goal Complete the Community Landmark AND achieve the Research Breakthrough before Day 60. Middle-ground endings await if you don't fully succeed. Press End Day → to advance.
