## The Story

The old world fell silent. No war. No warning. Just… *stillness*.

They called it **The Great Switch-Off** — a sudden, absolute disappearance of all electric power. No one knows why. No one knows if it can be undone. The cities emptied. The highways went quiet. And in the canopy of an ancient tree, a small group of survivors chose a different path.

You are their collective consciousness. Their shared voice. Their moral compass.

**This entire game was built by AI in a single conversation.** One prompt. One session. One file. No frameworks, no build steps, no dependencies. Just a question — *"what kind of game should we make?"* — and the answer that grew from it.

---

## What Is This?

A narrative simulation where you guide a post-apocalyptic community living in a massive tree. Every decision — who forages, who heals, whether to welcome a stranger or seal a poisoned spring — ripples through the **Bloom Score**, a living measure of your ecological harmony.

**How to play:** Click "Begin." Assign roles. End the day. Make hard choices. There is no tutorial because you don't need one.

---

## What's Inside

- 🌳 **Animated sanctuary** — A living canvas that responds to your Bloom Score with fireflies, falling leaves, and shifting light
- 👥 **Dynamic survivors** — 4+ characters with evolving traits shaped by the consequences of your decisions
- ⚖️ **14 moral dilemmas** — From tainted water to rival collectives, each with cascading effects and no clear right answer
- 🗺️ **Expedition system** — Send scouts to ruins, forests, and shores with narrative journal entries and mid-journey choices
- 🔨 **10 buildable structures** — Rain catchers, libraries, shrines — each changing the sanctuary's capabilities
- 🏛️ **Landmark win condition** — Build the Community Landmark to trigger a personalized epilogue based on every choice you made
- 🔊 **Procedural audio** — Gentle chimes, warnings, and day-transition tones generated in real-time
- 🌸 **Bloom Score** — The beating heart of the game. High bloom means abundance and beauty. Low bloom means short-term gain and long-term ruin.

All of it in **one HTML file**. No React. No Webpack. No npm install. Just open and play.
